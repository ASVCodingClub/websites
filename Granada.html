GRANADA

What can I visit?

Granada is a beautiful city where there are a lot of things to see. For me the most awesome monument is the alhambra of Granada with its Arabic decorations and its fountains. Another fantastic place is the Sacromonte is an ancient monastery where there are caves with passages that form a Via Crucis under the church

Food and drink

The operating system of Granada bars is very peculiar. When you order a beer they automatically give you a tapa. A tipically desert of Granada is the Pionono. Is a cake usually served rolled in a cylindrical sponge cake.

Place to stay

A lot of people think that the Parador of Granada is the most beautiful hotel in Granada. It is located inside of the Alhambra gardens.

Events

The May crosses are from May 3rd to May 5th.The main reason for the festival is to pay tribute to nature because May is the month of the beginning of the harvest season.
