New York tips

One World Trade Center

You should have your phone ready to record the fascinating elevator. While you are going up, it shows you the growth of the city since its beginnings.

Museum of Natural History

Probably the underground floor is the less visited, but if you like space, you should visit it. In that floor you can see an enormous meteorite.

Central park

If you like playing the piano, you can go to one of the pianos of central park and play it.
